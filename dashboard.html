How verdicts are decided

  • Keep — visited within ~30 days, multiple times.
  • Review — visited at least once but not for months, or never within history's reach.
  • Retire — bookmarked long ago, never opened (within recorded history), or untouched for 6+ months.
  • New — saved in the last fortnight; benefit of the doubt.

Chrome's history typically retains ~90 days. Older bookmarks you've used regularly may show fewer visits than reality. Treat this as a prompt for reflection, not a death sentence.
